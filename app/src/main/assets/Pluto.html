Pluto

Pluto is the ninth planet from the sun, and it is known as the dwarf planet in our solar system.The temperature is extremely cold, and it is so low to sustain any life there.

Let’s get started!

1) Pluto is the ninth planet from the sun.

2) It is a very small planet known as the dwarf planet in the solar system.

3) It has a thin atmosphere made of Chemicals like Nitrogen, Methane, and Carbon Monoxide.

5) It has five known moons: Charon, Styx, Nix, Kerberos, and Hydra.

6) Due to the extremely low temperature in Pluto, it is extremely difficult to sustain any life on the Planet.

7) It is so far from the sun that it receives 1/900th of the intensity of Sunlight we receive on Earth.

8) It is the farthest planet in the solar system.

9) Pluto’s surface is made of plains, mountains, valleys, and craters, and the plains are made of Frozen Nitrogen.

10) Pluto’s one year is equivalent to 248 years on Earth.
